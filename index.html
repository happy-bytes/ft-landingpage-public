Who are we?

As new parents, we were overjoyed to welcome a bundle of joy into our lives. However, distance posed a challenge in sharing these precious moments with our loved ones scattered across different platforms. Concerns about data privacy led us to seek an alternative to mainstream social media. Juggling between various apps for each family member felt overwhelming, and the fear of overlooking someone was all too real. Frustrated with the lack of a simple and private solution, we set out to create FamilyTime. A place where sharing the joy of our child's milestones could be both effortless and secure. FamilyTime is our answer to the need for a centralized, private, and user-friendly platform, designed with the understanding that every family deserves a dedicated space for their cherished moments.
